Car Scaping (Scrap warehouse)

A scrapyard or scrapyard is a cemetery for old and damaged cars, where serviceable parts are reused as spare parts, while damaged car bodies are melted down to be converted into pig iron after they are pressed.

Car graves

“Car graveyards” are places to store cars that were seized from their owners as evidence in cases, or because there was no data related to them, or because they were smuggled through customs, including stolen and illegal cars. The car remains in the “cemetery.” Experts estimate the value of unused cars forgotten in car cemeteries at more than tens of billions. They are wasted wealth that only thieves benefit from. Unlicensed vehicles and motorcycles are exported by the Traffic Department to their cemetery.
